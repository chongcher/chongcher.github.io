I had the privilege of delivering a talk titled "Legal AI Beyond the Demo" to a group of students and faculty members at the University of Science, Viet Nam National University Ho Chi Minh City ("VNU-HCM") on 29 June 2026.

I was impressed by the engagement and enthusiasm of the students during the presentation, as well as their thoughtful and insightful questions both before and after the discussion. It was also gratifying to see them continuing our conversation after the Q&A session.

I would like to express my appreciation to <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/lthoang">Dr Trung-Hoang Le</a> and team for their hospitality and graciousness in organizing the talk at short notice, and look forward to visiting VNU-HCM again.
